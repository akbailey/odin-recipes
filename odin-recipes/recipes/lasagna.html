Lasagna

Let me tell you, juggling three kids, a full-time job, and a never-ending pile of laundry is no joke. But somehow, in the midst of all that chaos, I still manage to throw together a lasagna that makes everyone think I’ve got my life together—at least for that one meal. This lasagna recipe? Oh, it’s an "heirloom," passed down from my Italian grandmother… except that Nonna always kept a jar of store-bought marinara in the pantry for emergencies, and let’s just say I’m not above doing the same.

Ingredients

  • Sauce
  • Ricotta
  • Flat pasta
  • Basil
  • Mozz

Directions

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Prepare a deep pan with oliveoil
  3. Put one layer of noodles down to cover the base of the pan
  4. Add a layer of Sauce
  5. Add a layer of Ricotta
  6. Top with another layer of pasta noodles and repeat until you reach the top of your pan
  7. Cover the top layer of noddels with mozzerella and put in oven at 375F for one hour
